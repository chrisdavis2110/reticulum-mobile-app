Larger image and file attachments:

• Images and files from other LXMF apps (Sideband, Columba) now come
  through at full resolution — attachments up to 4 MB are accepted,
  where the old limit silently dropped anything much larger and left
  an empty bubble
• Attachment files are stored off the message database, so a
  conversation full of photos opens fast and won't hit the size
  limit that could break the whole conversation view
• Large images decode downsampled in the timeline, so scrolling a
  photo-heavy chat stays smooth
