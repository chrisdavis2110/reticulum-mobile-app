• Experimental Reticulum Relay Chat (RRC): RRC hubs are now
  recognized on the Nodes tab and labelled "RRC hub" instead of
  showing as an unknown destination.
• When the experimental RRC feature is enabled, a discovered hub has
  a one-tap action on its Nodes row that adds it to the Rooms tab —
  no more copying destination hashes by hand.
• RRC remains off by default and under development. Ordinary
  one-to-one messaging is unaffected.
