• New experimental feature: Reticulum Relay Chat (RRC) rooms. Enable
  it in Settings, and a Rooms tab appears where you can add an RRC
  hub by its destination hash, connect, join rooms, and exchange
  messages. RRC is an IRC-style group-chat protocol layered on
  Reticulum links.
• RRC is off by default and still under development — it is not yet
  verified against a live hub. Ordinary one-to-one messaging is
  unaffected whether or not you turn it on.
