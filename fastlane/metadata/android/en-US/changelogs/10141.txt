• Fixed reactions and replies missing their target on relayed
  (group-chat) messages. Incoming messages that carried reply or
  reaction markers were assigned a message ID computed from a
  re-encoded copy of the payload instead of the bytes as received,
  so the ID could differ from what every other client and the
  forwarding service computed. A reaction or reply aimed at such a
  message then failed to attach to the right bubble.
• Plain text messages were unaffected; only messages with reply or
  reaction metadata diverged.
• No schema change. Receive-path message-ID computation only.
