• Identity private keys are now wrapped at rest with an Android
  Keystore-backed AES-256-GCM key. The wrapping key lives in the
  device's TEE (or StrongBox where available) and cannot be cloned
  to another device.
• Locked-phone forensic extraction no longer yields usable identity
  keys (the wrapping key requires device-unlocked state, API 28+).
• Existing installs migrate automatically on first launch — keys are
  re-saved through the vault and the plaintext columns are cleared.
• Closes the deeper HIGH-1 audit finding. The first-pass mitigation
  (Auto Backup disabled) was already in v1.1.25.
