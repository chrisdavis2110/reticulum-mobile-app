• Tap-back reactions — long-press any message bubble (after upgrade)
  to pick from a Signal-style emoji palette: 👍 ❤️ 😂 😮 😢 🙏.
  Reactions ship over LXMF field 16 (Sideband / Columba convention)
  so they interop with both Sideband and Columba peers — long-press
  a message FROM Sideband, tap an emoji, and Sideband shows your
  reaction natively.
• Aggregated reactions render as small `👍 2` chips below each
  bubble. Multiple senders' reactions to the same message combine
  on a single chip with a count.
• Schema migration to v11 adds the canonical LXMF message_id on
  every row sent or received after upgrade. Messages received
  before v1.1.33 don't have an id, so they can't be reacted to or
  replied to — anything new is fair game. The migration preserves
  all existing messages and contacts.
