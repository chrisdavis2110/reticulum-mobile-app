• Hotfix: v1.1.33 crashed at startup with "Migration didn't properly
  handle: messages" on devices upgrading from v1.1.32 or earlier
  (root cause: the v10→v11 migration created two indices that the
  @Entity annotation didn't declare, tripping Room's strict
  validator). The indices are now declared on the entity so the
  validator passes. Anyone affected on v1.1.33 had to wipe data;
  v1.1.34 upgrades cleanly.
• Swipe-right-to-reply — drag any bubble rightward past the
  threshold and the composer enters "Replying to <name>:
  <preview>" mode. The next Send packages the reply with LXMF
  field 16 `{"reply_to": <msg_id_hex>}`. Recipient renders the
  quote preview at the top of the reply bubble by looking up the
  target message locally. Wire-compatible with Sideband and
  Columba.
• A small quote-preview block appears above any received reply,
  showing the original sender and ≤80 chars of content. If the
  original message never arrived locally (out-of-order delivery,
  missed packet), the bubble shows a faded "Replying to a
  message…" fallback so the conversation thread stays
  comprehensible.
